How these counts are computed
  • Out of stock: parts with none left on hand.
  • Low stock: parts at or below the reorder level (the threshold to reorder).
  • Pending parts: parts a worker added in this hive that need a supervisor sign-off before Use / Restock unlocks.
  • Verdict: red if any out-of-stock for critical parts; amber if any low-stock OR pending; green when fully stocked.
