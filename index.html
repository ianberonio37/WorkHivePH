Why the hive is broken

Filipino workers are skilled.
The system is failing them.

Four systemic failures hold Filipino industry back. Not because of the workers. Because of the system.

Fragmentation

Every department runs in isolation. Every boundary requires a human to carry information, manually, imperfectly.

Knowledge Loss

When a technician retires, 12 years of expertise disappears entirely. Replacing that knowledge costs ₱800K to ₱2.4M in lost productivity per transition.

Reactive Processes

An unplanned failure costs 4 to 8 times more than the same repair done preventively. The warnings were there. No system was listening.

Skills Invisibility

Organizations can't deploy their best people because they don't know what their people can actually do. Jobs are assigned by availability, not competence.
