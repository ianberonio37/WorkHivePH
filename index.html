All WorkHive tools (free for every Filipino industrial worker)

  • Hive Live Board: Join or create a team. Hive is the unit of multi-tenancy: every worker, asset, and entry is scoped to one hive. Live activity feed + presence + role assignment.

  • Maintenance Day Planner: DILO / WILO / MILO / YILO multi-resolution personal scheduler. Pulls from your logbook entries and PM schedule.
  • Voice Journal: Speak in any of 10 Philippine languages (Tagalog, Cebuano, Ilocano, Hiligaynon, Bikol, Waray, Kapampangan, Pangasinan). Semantic recall + private rolling memory.
  • Resume / CV Builder: Turn your Skill Matrix, badges and logbook into a professional, ATS-friendly resume. Snap a photo or upload a PDF/Word/Excel from your phone. AI extracts the details into an editable checklist you control. Free for every worker.
  • AI Work Assistant: Floating helper on every page. Knows your hive, your role, your context. Routes through our AI service with memory + private-information redaction. Available across all 4 stages.
  • Skill Matrix: Discipline-by-level matrix across 5 industrial disciplines. Timed assessments with cooldown logic. Badges gate PM eligibility and project task assignment.
  • Report Sender: Shift handover + end-of-day digest. a-f structure with LOTO detection regex. Multi-channel email + print-ready PDF.
  • Alert Hub: One chronological inbox: risk, PM, stock, pattern, automation alerts. Plus the AMC Daily Brief at 06:00 PHT, a 5-sub-agent batched recommendation the supervisor approves with one tap.

  • Analytics Engine: MTBF, MTTR, Availability, OEE, PM Compliance, Pareto. 4 phases (descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, prescriptive). ISO 14224 + SMRP + ISA-101 anchored.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about WorkHive, pricing, privacy, and what works offline.

What is WorkHive?
WorkHive is a free industrial intelligence platform for Filipino industrial workers at every level: field technicians, supervisors, engineers, planners, and plant managers. It bundles a digital logbook, preventive maintenance scheduler, spare-parts inventory, skill matrix, engineering design calculators, and an AI work assistant in one browser-based hive.
Is WorkHive really free?
Yes. WorkHive is free at the worker tier forever. There is no credit card, no trial, and no per-user license. Plants advance through 4 free stages (Paper-to-Digital, Disciplined, Predictive-Ready, Industry Leader) before optional premium add-ons appear at the Industry Leader stage.
Who is WorkHive for?
WorkHive serves every role on the industrial floor: field technicians logging faults, supervisors approving shift handovers, planners scheduling PM, reliability engineers tracking MTBF, and plant managers reviewing OEE. It is built for Philippine factories, food and beverage facilities, power plants, water utilities, and any operation that runs industrial equipment.
What tools does WorkHive include?
The hive bundles a digital logbook, PM scheduler with overdue alerts, spare-parts inventory with FIFO and reorder points, skill matrix for technician competencies, engineering design calculators (electrical, mechanical, instrumentation, plumbing and fire, lighting, HVAC) referenced to ISO 14224 and Philippine standards, a day planner, and a Filipino/English AI work assistant.
Does WorkHive work offline?
Yes. WorkHive is a progressive web app (PWA). Once the page is loaded, the logbook, PM checklist, and inventory pages continue working offline. Entries are queued in browser IndexedDB and synced automatically when connectivity returns. This is critical for Philippine plants with intermittent network coverage.
Does WorkHive replace SAP, IBM Maximo, or my CMMS?
No. WorkHive does not replace your ERP or CMMS. It runs alongside them as the worker-facing layer your technicians actually use day to day. Plants with SAP or Maximo keep them for procurement and finance; WorkHive handles the shift handover, fault history, and PM execution that those systems are too heavy to do well.
How is my plant's data kept private?
Each plant gets an isolated hive with row-level security in Supabase. Workers can only see data from hives they have joined. Supervisors approve every new join request before access is granted. Data is hosted on Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.
Does WorkHive work on mobile?
Yes. WorkHive is mobile-first and installable as a PWA on Android and iPhone. The interface adapts from older Android phones with small screens up to desktop monitors in the control room. Touch targets, bottom navigation, and safe-area handling are tuned for industrial use with gloves and dirty screens.
What languages does WorkHive support?
English by default, with Filipino (Tagalog) support in the AI work assistant. Workers can ask questions in either language and receive responses appropriate to their role and stage. The Voice Journal additionally understands 10 Philippine languages, including Cebuano, Hiligaynon, and Ilocano.
How do I sign up?
Enter your work email in the Join the Hive form on this page. You will be added to the early-access list and contacted with a hive-join link once your facility is provisioned. Sign-up is free and takes about 30 seconds.
How do I start today, before my plant is provisioned?
Sign up free in under a minute, then open any tool in Solo Mode, no hive required. The Logbook, PM Scheduler, Engineering Calculators, and AI Assistant all run solo: type entries, run calculations, ask the assistant. When your plant joins, your Solo Mode entries migrate into your hive automatically. You do not have to wait for anyone to approve anything before getting value.
What if my plant still uses pen and paper logbooks?
That is the most common starting point in Philippine plants and the case WorkHive was built for. The Paper-to-Digital stage assumes nothing about your existing systems: no CMMS, no ERP, no sensors, no IT department. Start by digitizing one machine's logbook for one shift. We have a free guide at /learn/start-digital-logbook-philippine-factory/ that walks through a 12-week zero-budget rollout.
Can I use WorkHive on my own without my supervisor knowing?
Yes. Solo Mode is built specifically for workers who want to upskill quietly: OFW-track engineers building portable proof of work, new graduates learning industrial standards, technicians documenting their own repair history. Your Solo Mode data is private to you and never shared with any hive until you join one and explicitly migrate it. Solo Mode is private by design: the platform exists to protect Filipino workers' careers, not to surveil them.
How long until my plant sees results?
Expect 2 weeks for first signal, 90 days for measurable change, 12 months for compound returns. Week 1-2 the team builds the habit. Day 30 your first PM compliance number is real. Day 90 you can show a downtime trend. Month 6 the AI assistant gets useful because your logbook has enough entries to learn from. Month 12 you are at Disciplined stage and ready for Predictive-Ready features. Results track your data: the more your team logs, the sharper the insight becomes.
Will WorkHive run on my old Android phone with patchy data?
Yes. WorkHive targets the realistic Philippine plant-floor device: a 5-year-old Android phone with 3G data and a cracked screen, used with gloves. The app installs as a PWA in under 5 MB, the logbook works offline with IndexedDB queueing, touch targets are sized for gloved hands, the interface still loads on Chrome 90+. We test against the slowest devices on purpose because that is the real audience.
