How this shift plan is built
  • Top risk this shift: assets ranked by latest asset_risk_scores.risk_score; only high and critical bands shown (risk_score ≥ 0.70). Low/medium assets live in the full risk register, not the shift brief.
  • PMs due: frequency-aware overdue PMs (v_pm_scope_items_truth.is_overdue — next_due_date < today, derived from each task's own interval), one row per asset. Not the retired flat 30-day proxy.
  • Carry-forward: breakdown / corrective entries left Open from the prior shift.
  • Parts pre-stage: parts at or below min_qty that one of the prioritized jobs is likely to consume.
  • Verdict: red if 3+ top-risk OR 5+ carry-forward; amber if PMs due or 1+ top-risk; green when all four lists are short.
