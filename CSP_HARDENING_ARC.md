## Why platform-wide, not just index.html
`onclick=` inline handlers + the `cdn.tailwindcss.com` JIT-eval CDN are used on **every** page, not just
`index.html`. A strict CSP (no `unsafe-inline`, no `unsafe-eval`) breaks BOTH unless every inline handler
and inline `<script>` is migrated. So this arc lands the pattern on index.html first, then rolls it across
the ~40 tool pages (reuse `nav-hub.js`-style shared wiring where possible).
