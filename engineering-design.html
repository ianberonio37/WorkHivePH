Calculation Guide

HVAC Cooling Load - Simplified Total Heat Gain

Determines the total cooling requirement for a space based on all heat sources.

Q_total = Q_walls + Q_roof + Q_glass + Q_people + Q_equipment + Q_lighting + Q_infiltration
Q_walls = U × A_wall × ΔT
Q_roof = U × A_roof × ΔT × 1.15 (solar factor)
Q_glass = (0.57 × A × ΔT) + (A × SHGC × 200)
Q_people = N × (sensible + latent) W/person
Q_equipment = kW × 1000 × 0.85 (diversity factor)
Q_lighting = W/m² × floor area
Q_infiltration = 0.5 ACH × Volume × 0.35 × ΔT
Q_design = Q_total × 1.10 (safety margin)

Standards Applied

  • • PSME Code — Philippine Society of Mechanical Engineers
  • • ASHRAE Fundamentals Handbook (2021)
  • • ASHRAE Standard 62.1 — Ventilation for Acceptable Indoor Air Quality
  • • National Building Code of the Philippines (PD 1096)
  • • DOLE OSH Standards for workplace ventilation
