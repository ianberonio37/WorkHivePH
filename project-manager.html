How project health is computed
  • Active projects: active.
  • Past end date: past their end date but not yet closed: projects that should have closed but haven't.
  • On hold or planning: on hold or planning: work that's not moving yet.
  • Verdict: red if 3+ past end date; amber if any past end OR blocked count exceeds active; green when active > blocked and nothing overdue.
